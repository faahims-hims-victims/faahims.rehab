About the Site

FAA HIMS Reform Hub is a volunteer-driven resource for pilots navigating the challenges of the HIMS medical program. We focus on awareness, peer support, and legislative advocacy for a system based on medical science and empathy—not punishment.

Join the Forum

The heart of the community is the HIMS Victims Forum. Pilots share stories, get help, and organize for reform. This is your place to be heard and to connect with others who understand.

Stay Informed

Visit the blog for pilot interviews, policy analysis, and media coverage of HIMS-related issues. New posts are added regularly to support SEO and public awareness.
